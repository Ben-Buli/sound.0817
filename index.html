sounds we make, the way we listen

聲音與我們有著密切的關係，生活裡的音樂、自己發出來的聲音，隨意敲打的節拍，今年利澤聲音系列工作坊第四場，我們將帶大家透過即興創作、人聲探索、將生活中的物件變成樂器。

親愛的聲音，我們將透過三天的工作坊認識你，細細聆聽的過程中，我們將探索生活中會發生那些聲音，當我們過濾生活中複雜混亂的聲響，我們會聽見什麼？而我們又如何跟物件與空間互動，建立與聲音的對話？

這次我們邀請到聽說 Ting Shuo hear say 聲音藝術家Nigel Brown以及張惠笙，擔任本場次的帶路人，帶領我們透過「聲音」來探索自己、探索利澤。

    課程內容：
  1. 聆聽世界的方式
  2. 聲音藝術與實驗音樂是什麼？
  3. 即興創作的秘密：片刻裡的聆聽與回應
  4. 探索自己的聲音
  5. 樂器集合：製作生活裡的樂器
  6. 分享與聲音的交流

＃利澤聲音系列工作坊 ＃親愛的聲音 ＃Dearsound

sounds we make, the way we listen

This three-day workshop will explore what happens when we really focus our listening. What do we discover when we stop tuning-out the noises around us? How can we interact with the objects and spaces around us to create a conversation with sound?

    Sound artists Nigel Brown and Alice Hui-Sheng Chang from Ting Shuo Studio in Tainan will lead the group through listening and sound making sessions covering:
  1. Different ways of listening to the world
  2. Sound art and experimental music: what is it?
  3. Improvisation: listening and responding in the moment
  4. Human voice: how well do you know yours?
  5. Instruments: what can be an instrument and how can I make one?
  6. Performance: how can we make a meaningful experience to share with others?

We will answer these questions together by listening to examples and creating sounds ourselves inside and outside in various places around Lize. Over three days we will build up a bank of experiences and recordings to play with in a final group sound composition on the last day.
